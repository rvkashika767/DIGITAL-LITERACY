Social media checklist

Do's 
[  ] Private accounts 
[  ] 2-step authentication 
[  ] Report spam/ nsfw /false info
[  ] Think before posting anything 
[  ] Separate personal and professional posts 

Don'ts 
[  ] Don't share password/ details
[  ] Don't accept requests from people you don't know 
[  ] Don't post any private information - bank details , exact address 
[  ] Don't spread misinformation 
[  ] Don't indulge in any activity/ comments that can lead to legal action
